About Us

Spade is a dynamic community of economics enthusiasts at our college, dedicated to applying economic principles to real-world challenges. We foster intellectual growth through workshops, discussions, and collaborative projects, aiming to bridge the gap between theory and practice. Our members engage in diverse activities, from analyzing market trends to developing innovative solutions for economic issues. Join us to explore the practical side of economics and make a tangible impact.
